Delete Your Account

You can delete your Notes: Simple, Smooth account and all associated data at any time. Here's how:

Option 1: Delete directly in the app (instant)

  1. Open the Notes app on your device
  2. Tap the ... button in the top right corner of the app
  3. Tap Delete account
  4. Confirm the deletion

Your account and all your notes, images, and data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us. We will process your request within 7 days.

Email us to delete your account
⚠️ This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted

  • Your email address and account credentials
  • All notes, text, and images you have created
  • All account metadata and timestamps

We do not retain any backup copies of your data after deletion.
