Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

NotesApp ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the NotesApp service:

  • Email address — used to create and authenticate your account.
  • Note content — titles, text, and images you add to your notes.
  • Account metadata — timestamps for when notes are created, modified, or deleted.

We do not collect your name, location, device identifiers, usage analytics, or any other personal information beyond what is listed above.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and maintain the NotesApp service:

  • To authenticate you and secure your account.
  • To store, sync, and display your notes across your sessions.
  • To send account-related emails (password reset, email confirmation).

We never sell, rent, or share your personal information or note content with third parties for any purpose, including advertising.

Third-Party Services

NotesApp uses Supabase (supabase.com) to provide secure database storage, authentication, and file storage infrastructure. Your data is stored on Supabase servers. Supabase's privacy practices are described at supabase.com/privacy. We do not use any advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Data Retention

  • Notes you delete are moved to "Recently Deleted" and permanently purged after 30 days.
  • When you delete your account, all your notes, images, and profile data are permanently and immediately removed from our servers.
  • We do not retain any backup copies of your data after account deletion.

Security

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS and encrypted at rest by our infrastructure provider. Access to your notes is protected by Row-Level Security policies that ensure only your account can read or modify your data. Session tokens are stored securely using the device's encrypted keychain.

Children's Privacy

NotesApp is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13 years of age. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your data at any time. You can delete your account and all associated data directly from Settings → Delete account. For any other data requests, contact us at the address below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
